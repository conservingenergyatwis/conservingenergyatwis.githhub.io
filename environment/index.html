Environmental Issues

Our energy mainly relies on non-renewable energy, which, could cause a lot of environmental issues as using fossil fuels leads into a more serious climate issue.
(Image source: MrGreenScience)
After generating energy, carbon dioxide is released into the air, which gets re-radiated and could not exit the atmosphere and gets reflected back to earth. The entire process repeats and more carbon dioxide is accumulating within the atmosphere, which causes climate change to happen. Winter isn't cold anymore, summer is a lot hotter than before, some countries even lost seasons, with only summer remaining.
