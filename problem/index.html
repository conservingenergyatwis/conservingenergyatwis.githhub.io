The Problem

We use or consume different types of energy everyday, such as electrical energy, chemical energy.
Let's focus on electrical energy.
The cost of energy has been rising since mid 2021, to it's highest point during October of 2021 (or you could say it has been rising slowly for a long time, inflation, right?). Why? Could be due to multiple factors, such as reduce in available power supplies compared to before, higher natural gas prices and so on. All these different factors lead to the rising costs of energy.
Conserving Energy
It is an urgent need to conserve energy. Why? Because of how much energy that are made with non-renewable energy, and at this rate, we could run out of raw material such as coal, natural gas and others to produce non-renewable energy.
